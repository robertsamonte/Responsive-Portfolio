About Me


Thank you for visiting my website.

My name is Robert Samonte. I am a licensed Realtor affiliated with Global Brokers Realty, Inc. located in Orlando, Florida.

My focus is on sales, purchase, and leasing opportunities in the Orlando market area. I work with Owners and Buyers of Residential, and Investment properties.

Aside from the CIPS designation, I am also a NAR Green designee and has e-PRO certification.

I hold a B.S. Economics from University of Santo Tomas and an MBA degree from Lake Forest Graduate School in Illinois. I am fluent in English, and Filipino (Tagalog) languages.
